In computer-based text processing and digital typesetting, a non-breaking space or no-break space (NBSP) is a variant of the space character that prevents an automatic line break (line Wrap) at its position. In certain formats (such as HTML), it also pre- vents the "collapsing" multiple consecutive whitespace charac- ters into a single space.
